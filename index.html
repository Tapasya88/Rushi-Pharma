Sourcing - INDIA

    Source evaluation is done thoroughly as below :
  • Visit / Verification of the real manufacturing site and coordinates
  • Product licence / Certifications available to the mfg. site
  • Sourcing product status: campaign / regular/ made against order + inhouse standard specs /MOA.
  • Check whether they have basic GMP like systems.
  • Product price history for past one year to six months.
  • Installed capacity and % usage of the capacity at present.
  • Check if the source has been audited by any company/agency
  • Tech support with documents and Regulatory status
  • We provide Samples, Pre-shipment samples, Third Party Analysis if reqd.
  • We provide updates on the latest products from the source.
